Attending the 96th Annual Meeting of the Transportation Research Board

Last updated on Oct 25, 2017 1 min read

The 96th Annual Meeting of the Transportation Research Board (TRB) was held on January 8 – 12 2017, at the Walter E. Washington Convention Center, in Washington, D.C. – USA. The spotlight theme for the 2017 TRB Annual Meeting is Transportation Innovation: Leading the Way in an Era of Rapid Change. The program attracted more than 12,000 transportation professionals from around the world to Washington, D.C.

Meixin Zhu gave an oral presentation on car-following model calibration and validation.
